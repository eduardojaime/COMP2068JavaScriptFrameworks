What are the benefits of using packages?
- Save time, and effort
- Allows you to expand the features of your app
- Reusable components can be used across your apps
- In industry projects, most of the time, there's not enough time to build things from scratch
- Packages that have current support, will provide updates

What's the downside of using packages?
- If packages are removed/deleted they can break your app
- Dependency on an external team of developers
- Cannot be customized (easily)
- Security vulnerabilities (use tools to scan your packages)
- Some may not be maintained regularly
